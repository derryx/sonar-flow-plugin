The first step in a "catch"-sequence should be an invoke of pub.flow:getLastError.

If the first step in the "catch"-sequence is not pub.flow:getLastError you might not get the correct error message that triggered your catch.

Noncompliant Code Example

  Invoke a service other than pub.flow:getLastError as first step in your "catch"-sequence.

Compliant Solution

  Add pub.flow:getLastError as first step in your "catch"-sequence.
